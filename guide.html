How to use MOWES

My Own Weekly Exercise Sheet — the complete guide

MOWES helps you plan and track your workouts, right from your phone. It's free, there's no sign-up, and everything stays on your device. Here's everything you can do — it's simpler than it looks. 🙂

1
📅 Pick your day

Your whole week runs across the top. Today sits in the middle with a small dot. Swipe left or right, or just tap a day to jump to it.

2
➕ Add an exercise

Tap + Add routine, then type the name — like Push-ups or Morning walk.

3
🔢 Track it your way

On each exercise, tap a chip to add what you want to track:

  • + Reps — add sets and type your reps. Tap Done when a set is finished.
  • + Time — gives you a built-in timer (see step 4).
  • + Weight — note the weight you used.
  • + Distance — add miles, great for runs or walks.
4
⏱️ The timer — the star feature

Type the minutes and press ▶ → it counts down to 0:00.
Leave it blank and press ▶ → it counts up like a stopwatch.
When it's done, the box turns green ✓.

5
🔁 Copy a day

Built a good day and want it on others too? Tap Copy to…, pick the days, and tap Copy.

6
↕️ Reorder & remove

Use the ▲ ▼ arrows, or press & hold a card to drag it. Tap the red ✕ to delete an exercise.

7
📖 Make pages

Tap ＋ Page to add pages like At the Gym or At Home. Tap a page's name to rename it, and swipe the tabs to flip between pages.

8
🕘 Look back

Tap 🕘 History any time to see past weeks and everything you did.

9
💾 Saved & private — automatically

Everything saves by itself, right on your phone. No sign-up, no account — nothing is ever sent anywhere.

10
📲 Install it like an app

In your phone's browser, tap Share → Add to Home Screen. Now MOWES opens like a real app — even with no internet.

🔒 Your privacy comes first

No sign-up, no account, no email. Your data stays on your phone — it's never sent anywhere. Free, and works offline.
