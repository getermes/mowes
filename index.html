    How to use MOWES

    A quick tour. It's simpler than it looks — tap around and you can't break anything. 🙂

    1
    📅 Pick your day

    Your week runs across the top. Today sits in the middle with a little dot. Swipe left or right, or just tap a day to jump to it.

    2
    ➕ Add an exercise

    Tap + Add routine, then type the name — like Push-ups or Morning walk.

    3
    🔢 Track it your way

    On each exercise, tap a chip to add what you want:

    • + Reps — add sets and type your reps. Tap Done when a set is finished.
    • + Time — gives you a built-in timer (see step 4).
    • + Weight — note the weight you used.
    • + Distance — add miles (great for runs or walks).
    4
    ⏱️ The timer — the star feature

    Type the minutes and press ▶ → it counts down to 0:00.
    Leave it blank and press ▶ → it counts up like a stopwatch.
    When it's done, the box turns green ✓.

    5
    🔁 Copy a day

    Built today's plan and want it on other days too? Tap Copy to…, pick the days, and tap Copy.

    6
    ↕️ Reorder & remove

    Use the ▲ ▼ arrows, or press & hold a card to drag it. Tap the red ✕ to delete an exercise.

    7
    📖 Make pages

    Tap ＋ Page to add pages like At the Gym or At Home. Tap a page's name to rename it. Swipe the tabs to flip between pages.

    8
    🕘 Look back

    Tap 🕘 History any time to see past weeks and everything you did.

    9
    💾 Saved & private — automatically

    Everything saves by itself, right on your phone. No sign-up, no account — nothing is ever sent anywhere.

    10
    📲 Install it like an app

    In your phone's browser, tap Share → Add to Home Screen. Now MOWES opens like a real app — even with no internet.
